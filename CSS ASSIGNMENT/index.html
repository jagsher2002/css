"Breathin" by Ariana Grande

"Breathin" by Ariana Grande is a song that many people can relate to because of its message about overcoming inner demons and challenges. The lyrics talk about feeling overwhelmed by negative feelings like despair and anxiety, but finding strength to persevere and keep breathing. This message can be even more impactful than advice from friends or family because it is inspiring and realistic. For people with mental disorders, this message provides special encouragement to overcome difficulties. Ariana's strong voice and uplifting melody further emphasize the song's message, making it a source of support and inspiration for many."
